Natural Language processing and chatterbots:

As the world is experiencing a rapid increase of population and a fast development of technology, the data that needs to be collected and processed inherently becomes heavier. Therefore, the world has developed an artificial intelligence program that can understand, collect, and make use of large amounts of data from human language, including two most prominent forms, speech and text, this is called Natural Language Processing, or NLP in short. There are many uses of NLP in the world, Google Translate could be an example for it. The computer can collect data from a sentence of a language, and translate that sentence into any different languages. Basically, NLP is the ability given to computers so that they can perform human works, which was impossible for them to do in the past. As NLP is developing at a fast speed, many individuals and organizations have started applying NLP in many different fields, including Chatbots to collect data, improve customer engagement, and increase profits.

Functions:

Different from the past, when chatbots could only process simple data and answer simple questions, chatbots nowadays can answer some questions which are way more complicated thanks to the application of NLP. According to statistics from IBM, 80% of normal daily questions can be answered easily by chatbots, the other 20% require higher training activities (Jovic 2020). However, this is enough to prove that chatbots can now answer almost all of the normal questions that in the past, only humans could do. By showing their usefulness to the modern world, chatbots are now being more and more implied and used by organizations. Specifically, statistics from Aqquire shows that more than 1.4 billion people use chatbots for daily tasks, proving their popularity (Jovic 2020). Besides the “classic” text-to-text interaction between humans and chatbots, speech-to-text or speech-to-speech could also be the ways to communicate with them. These ways of communication allow the bots to work as virtual assistants, rather than just question-and-answers bots. This has been applied to many devices with different operating systems and organisations, such as Alexa for Amazon, Siri for Apple, Bixby for Samsung or Cortana for Microsoft. Not only translating languages or answering text questions, they can understand human speech and from there manipulate the devices to perform daily tasks such as create schedules, set alarms, check emails and so on. Overall, chatterbots now have developed beyond their own “chatterbots” terminology. However, they still have downsides, such as giving wrong information, or not understanding customers' needs. This sounds very normal with individuals and small corporations, but can be a real problem with the big ones because it will not only affect customer engagement, but also drag down the reputation of the companies. Therefore, researching and improving NLP is also one of the top priorities of organisations in order to provide more accurate information for the end-users. In the near future, chatbots have the potential to develop even further. Since the social media channels are becoming more popular, chatbots inherently, will be applied to their system. Facebook Messenger, Instagram or Whatsapp have been great environments for bots to thrive. Furthermore, with the effort of improving NLP quality, chatbots in the near future can become more sentimental and understandable in different users' contexts and perhaps different users’ languages (Kelley 2019). Last but not least, the improvement in artificial intelligence and machine learning is crucial in order to create not only smart, but also trendy and trustworthy chatbots.

The thrivingness of chatbots affects many fields of life. Since they can work 24/7, after asking questions, customers can receive the answers immediately. Due to the increase in customer engagement, chatbots have saved billions of dollars for many organizations, especially financial institutions such as banks. In 2019, chatbots have saved 127 million dollars just in the bank sector (Martin 2020). In 2022, as a result of the increase in chatbots popularity, they are expected to save businesses more than 8 billion dollars (Deloitte n.d). With the development of messaging apps, people will not only be able to ask questions through those apps, but also do more higher-level actions such as making bank payments. Moreover, the improvement in NLP and AI technology is going to be a great contribution, chatbots will be able to listen better. As a result, this could change people’s behaviour. Instead of paying by using credit cards, SMS or QR codes, people would be able to pay for their products just by some simple conversations with bots on Facebook Messenger or any other messaging apps. In the future, besides the financial advantages that chatbots can provide, many other sectors such as education and healthcare, the sectors that require fast answers, would also be beneficial from the bots as well. For education, chatbots have been working as teaching assistants, and with the great potential of NLP, they shall further understand not only one, but multiple languages. Therefore, chatbots will be able to individually support students from different countries and on a larger scale (Winkler, Rainer & Söllner, Matthias 2018). Chatbots could also assist hospitals and patients since they can help booking appointments, recording patient profiles or making medical diagnosis. Cybersecurity and privacy:

As our world is being reshaped by digital technologies, the status quo will continue to be disrupted. Technological advancements can lead to greater societal and economic benefits, but they also bring new security challenges. Defending networks, computers, and programs from digital attacks is called cybersecurity. Most of these attacks are targeted at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive data; extorting money from users; or disrupting normal business operations.Because there are more devices than people, and attackers are increasingly innovative, it can be challenging to implement effective cybersecurity measures. Multiple layers of protection are used as part of a successful cybersecurity approach. Those layers protect computers, networks, programs, and data. To create an effective defense against cyber attacks, people, processes, and technology must complement each other(Cisco nd) There are a variety of prominent, emerging, and resurgent cybersecurity risks and threats currently, so a new form of securing and protecting your data online has been created: cybersecurity and privacy. There are many forms of cybersecurity: infrastructure security(traffic light,...),application security(antivirus app,...), network security(password, application security), cloud security( Cloud is becoming popular due to improved cyber security), and IoT security( sensors, camera,...).(Mindcore 2018)

Functions:

One of the biggest changes in the cyber security industry over the past 20 years has been its ubiquity.There was no mainstream industry for cyber security just 10 years ago. It was generally believed that only large organizations could be targets and that there was no such position as Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)."It was a space that barely existed a decade ago, and what was accessible was uninteresting, unscientific, and ineffective," Cybsafe creator Oz Alashe concurred.In the previous half-century, cryptography has gone a long way; the discovery of the RSA algorithm in the 1970s was a watershed moment. Encryption and decryption may now be done with separate keys thanks to RSA. It also completely transformed the field.(Logan 2019) The dominant idea in the cyber security business for a long time was to add more obstacles – more layers of complexity – to technology. It was argued that cyber criminals would have a difficult time breaking into sophisticated systems with multiple barriers to entry."[It] was predominantly seen as a forensic and reactive industry," Red Sift co-founder Randal Pinto says of historical cyber security. The solutions were costly, and they relied on paying consultants to look into and remedy the faults that led to a cyber attack.Increasingly in contemporary cyber security, psychology and behavioural science is as important as coding and adversaries get more creative and sophisticated in their methods. Cybsafe, a member of the Cyber 1.0 cohort, provide the world’s first intelligent cyber security awareness, behaviour and culture risk management solution, fusing psychology and behavioural science with artificial intelligence and data science(Logan 2019)

Blockchain and Cryptocurrency

As the emergence of technology and digital information, the majority of day-to-day activities are functioned by the apex of robotic and high-end technology. While traditional money has several insecure disadvantages, blockchain and cryptocurrency becomes a vital transaction globally contributing to a thriving economy in a virtual way.

Functions:

What is cryptocurrency? Cryptocurrency is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system which does not need verification from the bank in a transaction. The first digital cash coined by Satoshi Nakamoto is bitcoin and is still the most traded recently. The application of cryptocurrency is to utilize the transmitting process’s security, low cost of transfers and non-time deferring, and with the online database storing like blockchain, digital money cannot be more secure. So, what is blockchain? From my experience, blockchain is a chain of blocks that store the information and multiple hosted accounts. A new block created when a customer has a transaction, it will record the history and store it in a block, and it keeps doing the same routine. The block connects to another by a ‘chain’, technically it is called a hash, created by an algorithm in order to make it unique. The hash is also a key factor to open and modify the information inside the blockchain framework. This makes blockchain impossible to be invaded and scammed by hackers because if one change is made without verification from the host, the whole system will be alerted. There are also Miners who build a new block on chain, the mining process is complicated due to the algorithm hashes are required to be before a new block is added in. After that, miners will receive their rewards financially. The most crucial technology of blockchain is decentralization, which means there is no individual or organization owning the blockchain. The host information of the block will be connected to the chain via the nodes which could be any technology that stores copies of the blockchain and keeps it working. With the breakthrough of blockchain and cryptocurrency, the needs of third parties are no longer involved between the two who are transacting, this solves the long and complicated process of trading. Every user can get a copy of Blockchain, a distributed database, and add new records as long as the permission is still valid. This proves a true work due to the unchangeable data inside the block and when combined with cryptocurrency, it creates a secure and high-speed transmitting playground for investors and highly demanding privacy occupations involved. Part 5: Project Ideas

Our project is about to create an open world game, which can be manipulated not only by devices, but also voice commands. In order to make the game function well, we divided the tasks for different teams, each team would carry a part of the game. There is a programming and designing team, a data team, and a security team.

Design team :

The design game position will be in charge of every aspect of design which may be different based on what game the team is going to make. There are several kinds of design, with lower game requirements, the positions are game design and level design. With the project we would like to do in future, we must have a system designer, gameplay designer, mission designer and openworld designer,... The person who is a game designer is responsible for planning new mechanics and framework in game, after the approval from other members, this member will make it more specific. The level designers take the idea from the game designer, then create the map and structures in the game. The ‘’greybox’’ is a technology that we use to make a level, in which we can be creative by combining blocks and other material to make the building more realistic. During the process, we must have strong bonding communication and encourage each other, the team must know more about each other and think the same thing so that we can design in the most productive mode and better the experience for players. To make an open world game, the design team who are in charge of making characters and maps will use Blender, this software allows users to create realistic figures and building structures; Furthermore, Blender also slightly supports in the field of animation. Emacs will be the tool of system design where the team can function the animations, interactions and transmitting data by coding.

Data team:

This team will collect data from the users. Particularly, a server is created to store the data from costumes, skins, character customizations, artefacts, enemies, quests and most importantly, the user IDs, which are the primary key that differentiate them with each other. Furthermore, the user data is collected everyday to improve personalization. Voice commands which are most used, are also stored. After the collecting process is completed, each week, the characters that are used will be upgraded to make them more insightful, sentimental and realistic. About the tool that will be used, Javascript and C++ will be used to collect data from both device interaction, which are the commands from keyboards and mouses, and voice interaction, collected from voice commands. The data will be stored by SQL in order to further upgrade the characters. Also, just like other games, we want to collect user feedback as well as the game’s downloads, to find out problems of the game, and this is also stored by SQL as well. If the project is successful, half of the profit will be donated to charity to help poor people.

Security team:

For the security aspect of the game, we must ensure that the accounts of every player are protected and can be recovered. To do this, when the player create their account, they must enter the email address and phone number so that when they lost their account due to hacker or they just simply forget their password, they can use email or phone number to change password back(For Example, when they click recover password an OTP code will be send to their email or phone, and by enter that code, they can create the new password).Moreover, when the player create their account we must use encryption method(like RSA) to encrypt the player password and store it with the player username in our database so that even when hacker can somehow access to our database, they can not get any information. We will also inform players frequently about the fake website of the game through mail and remind players about the scam through text that sometimes appears in the corner of the screen. Players can report other players about the bad behavior( especially scam) so we can block them and protect our community. We will use Denuvo - a game protection and Anti-Privacy technology helping game publishers and developers to secure PC, console and mobile games to prevent people from cracking or hacking our game. By doing this we will make the players in our community feel safe and enjoy playing our game.
